Visit American Fork

Enjoy food, shopping, and a plethora of exciting outdoor adventures and sports in this quiet yet busy town in Northern Utah

The top three activities to do in American Fork

Steel Days

Shop at the farmers market, ride thrilling roller coasters at the carnival, watch the parade, and much more.

Timpanogos Cave

Go on a scenic hike and follow it up with a tour of this incredible national monument.

Art Dye Park

Enjoy a walk, visit the dog park, or play sports like pickleball, baseball, soccer, and frisbee golf.
